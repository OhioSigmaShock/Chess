The History of Chess

Chess originated in India around the 6th century, known as Chaturanga. It spread to Persia, becoming Shatranj, and then to Europe during the Middle Ages, where it evolved into the modern game we know today.

  • Bobby Fischer
  • Magnus Carlsen
  • José Raúl Capablanca
  • Anatoly Karpov
